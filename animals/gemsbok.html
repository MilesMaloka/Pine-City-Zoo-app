Gemsbok

The gemsbok is a large antelope native to arid regions of Southern Africa. Recognized by its striking black-and-white facial markings and long, straight horns, the gemsbok is well adapted to desert life. It can survive for long periods without water, obtaining moisture from the plants it eats. Gemsbok live in herds and are known for their strength, speed, and ability to defend themselves against predators.
